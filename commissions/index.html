Hi, I'm HamHamBone and I do fantasy map commissions!

My Art

I've always loved to draw maps of imaginary places, it is one of my favorite hobbies! I'm dipping my toes into offering commissions to draw fantasy world and region maps for D&D games and other similar things. I'll work with you or off of a provided sketch to create a cool looking map!

I specialize in regional maps, look elsewhere for location, dungeon, or city maps.

All work will be digitally sketched, inked, and colored.
